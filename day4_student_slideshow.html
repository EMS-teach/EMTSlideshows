üì¶ CHALLENGE 1: MEDICAL SUPPLY RELAY

TIME LIMIT: 8 minutes

RULES:

  • Only TEAM LEADER can speak
  • Organize supplies by category: Airway, Trauma, Cardiac, Assessment
  • Team leader gives verbal instructions ONLY
  • No touching supplies until leader directs
SUCCESS = Correct categories + Effective leadership

üìã CHALLENGE 2: PATIENT INFORMATION CHAIN

TIME LIMIT: 8 minutes

RULES:

  • Line up single file
  • First person gets patient info card
  • Relay information verbally through ENTIRE team
  • NO WRITING allowed
  • Last person tells instructor the information
GOAL: >80% accuracy in patient details
